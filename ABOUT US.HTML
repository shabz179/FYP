ABOUT US

At Bubble and Waffle, we love making desserts that bring a smile to your face. Our specialty is bubble waffles, which are crispy on the outside and fluffy on the inside. You can choose from a variety of toppings, like fresh fruits, chocolate, ice cream, and sauces, to create your perfect dessert.

We are proud to be part of the Dagenham community and are committed to providing friendly service. Our team is here to make sure you have a great experience every time you visit.

Our bubble waffles are made fresh just for you, guaranteeing every bite is warm, crispy, and delightfully fluffy! Dive into a world of toppings—choose from juicy fruits, luscious chocolates, creamy ice creams, and indulgent sauces to create your dream dessert. Whether you crave something classic or want to try something new, we have the perfect treat to satisfy your sweet tooth!

Come and enjoy a tasty treat at Bubble and Waffle. We can’t wait to serve you!
